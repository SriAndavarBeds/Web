Our Products

Explore our handcrafted sleep collections, tailored for every budget and posture preference

Traditional Natural

Elavam Panji Pillows and Mattress

Experience the comfort of nature

Elavam Panji (Silk Cotton) is a traditional natural filling material known for its soft texture, lightweight feel, and natural cooling properties.

Benefits:
  • Made from 100% natural silk cotton
  • Excellent air circulation & body-cooling
  • Soft yet supportive sleep surface
  • Eco-friendly and chemical-free
  • Lightweight and highly breathable
Perfect for: Families, traditional homes, and customers looking for natural, organic sleeping comfort.
Traditional Firm

Paruthi Panji Pillows and Mattress

Natural softness with firm support

Paruthi Panji (Cotton) mattresses provide firm, organic skeletal support and unmatched breathability, loved across generations.

Benefits:
  • 100% natural cotton filling
  • Provides firm body & back support
  • Helps maintain correct sleeping posture
  • Absorbs moisture naturally for cool sleep
  • Skin-friendly and easy to maintain
Perfect for: Elders, daily use, posture-conscious customers, and traditional homes.
Modern Ortho

Foam Pillows and Mattress

Modern comfort for everyday living

Premium high-density foam mattresses designed to deliver balanced body support, pressure relief, and long-lasting recovery.

Benefits:
  • Excellent localized body support
  • Relieves pressure on back and shoulders
  • Lightweight, easy to clean & handle
  • High-density foam that retains shape
  • Available in custom thickness profiles
Perfect for: Modern apartments, hostels, budget hotels, and daily family use.
Luxury Hotel

Spring Pillows and Mattress

Luxury comfort with advanced support

Advanced spring suspension designs that offer premium posture alignment, zero partner disturbance, and five-star luxury hotel feel.

Benefits:
  • Five-star luxury sleeping comfort
  • Optimal spinal alignment & distribution
  • Minimal partner motion disturbance
  • Superior cross-ventilation system
  • Heavy-duty structure built for longevity
Perfect for: Premium bedrooms, luxury hotels, resorts, and premium comfort seekers.
